- **Collaborate with AI/ML researchers** to translate AI concepts into tangible prototypes.  
- Contribute to **groundbreaking research** while gaining hands-on experience in cutting-edge AI technologies and software engineering practices.  

This role offers the unique opportunity to be at the forefront of Apple Intelligence integration, using Photos as the ideal playground of personal data to pioneer next-generation AI experiences.
